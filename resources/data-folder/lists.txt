unorder list
disc (default): Filled circle bullet point.
circle: Hollow circle bullet point.
square: Square bullet point.

order list
1: Arabic numerals (default).
A: Uppercase alphabetical letters (A, B, C, ...).
a: Lowercase alphabetical letters (a, b, c, ...).
I: Uppercase Roman numerals (I, II, III, ...).
i: Lowercase Roman numerals (i, ii, iii, ...).
